## Implementation decisions grounded in those references

- Unit 1 covers Period 1, **1491–1607**, has **4–6%** suggested AP Exam weighting, and includes topics 1.1 through 1.7.
- Multiple-choice practice remains stimulus-based; APUSH uses historical texts, interpretations, images, graphs, and maps.
- The game’s writing system preserves the AP History scoring structures:
  - SAQ: 3 points
  - LEQ: 6 points
  - DBQ: 7 points
- Beginning with the May 2027 exam, all SAQs include a source and the LEQ changes its choice structure; the official scoring rubrics remain unchanged.
